What's Turms?
-
Turms is a program for OS X and iOS that allows you to prepare and publish your things on blogs and social networks.

Who's Turms?
-
Turms was the Etruscan god of trade and the messenger god between people and gods.

What does?
-
Start collectiong your thoughts, wherever you are. Collect photos, links and audio clips. All your data will be seamlessly synced via iCloud to every device. Don't forget anymore the thing you was thinking about this morning, when, in the evening, you are on your mac writing this clever post.
